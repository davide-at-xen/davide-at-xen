About Us

Building the future of secure communication

Xen Quantum is an early-stage deep tech startup on a mission to make quantum-secure communication accessible and scalable. As quantum computers advance, today's encryption standards face an existential threat. We are building the hardware to ensure that sensitive data remains protected — not just now, but forever.

Founded by a team of quantum physicists and engineers, we are developing compact, silicon-based quantum communication modules that integrate into existing network infrastructure. Our approach combines the fundamental security guarantees of quantum physics with the manufacturability and scalability of silicon photonics.

Technology

Silicon-based quantum communication

Our modules leverage the principles of quantum key distribution to enable provably secure communication channels. By building on silicon photonics, we make quantum security practical and deployable at scale.

Quantum Key Distribution

Generate and distribute encryption keys with security guaranteed by the fundamental laws of quantum mechanics. Any eavesdropping attempt is immediately detectable.

Silicon Photonics

Our modules are built on silicon photonic integrated circuits, enabling compact form factors, low power consumption, and compatibility with standard semiconductor manufacturing.

Network Integration

Designed from the ground up for seamless integration with existing fibre-optic networks and telecom infrastructure, minimising deployment complexity.
